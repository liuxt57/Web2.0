Pie

INTRODUCTION

Pie is a kind of pastry food which can be divided into single-skin pie and double-skin pie. They are usually made of flour, butter, sugar, etc. They are made into dough, which is a kind of crisp and layered snacks by rolling, shaping, ripening and decorating.

DIFFERENT KINDS OF PIE

  • Apple Pie:Apple pie may be the most famous one, which was first originated in Europe, but now becomes a typical American style food.According to "The Dictionary of American Food and Drink", the popularity of apple pie has made the United States the largest apple producer in the world.
    Apple pie has a variety of shapes, sizes and tastes, including freestyle, standard two-tier, caramel apple pie, French apple pie, crumb apple pie, sour cream apple pie, to name a few.
